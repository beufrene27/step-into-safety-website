About Me

Step Into Safety helps homeowners and caregivers convert existing bathtubs into accessible walk‑in tubs. Our mission is to provide a cost‑effective alternative to full bathroom remodels by cutting and fitting a waterproof insert into your tub. We pride ourselves on quality workmanship, fast installations, and compassionate service that empowers seniors and anyone with mobility challenges to bathe safely.

Our Process

We specialize in converting your existing bathtub into a safe, accessible walk‑in tub. From the initial cut to the final seal, our trained technicians handle every step with precision and care. Here’s how it works:

Cutting the Tub

We carefully remove a portion of your existing tub to create a low‑entry threshold.

Installing the Insert

A waterproof insert is fitted and sealed, ensuring a secure and watertight step‑in opening.

Customer Consultation

Our team walks you through all available options, including an optional watertight door.

Enjoy Your Walk‑In Tub

Once complete, enjoy safe and comfortable access to your tub—no costly renovation required!
